About Me:

Hi there! My name's Casey Kennedy. Thanks for checking out my page. I used to work in the performing entertainment industry back when I was living in the beautiful and sunny Philippines. I was very fortunate to have experienced and taken part in so much. While I enjoyed all the projects I worked on and art that was made, I took a break to pursue my college education, after which I started a successful business in healthcare. I worked a few years as a Flight Attendant to see more of this wonderfully vast world...
... Now I live in Sweden and am looking to recover my love for music. Whether you're looking for a singer, or someone to collaborate with... send me a message and we can talk.
